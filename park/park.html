Kot Bury Ferm
Looking for a fun-filled day out with family and friends? Look no further than Kot Bury Ferm, the premier amusement park in the region! Located in the heart of the countryside, Kot Bury Ferm offers something for everyone. Thrill-seekers can get their adrenaline pumping on our roller coasters, while younger children can enjoy our gentle rides and attractions. With a variety of games, shops, and food stalls, there's never a dull moment at Kot Bury Ferm. But that's not all – Kot Bury Ferm is also home to a range of farm animals, so visitors can get up close and personal with horses, cows, chickens, and more. And if you're feeling particularly adventurous, why not try your hand at goat milking or egg collecting? Our park is designed with your safety in mind, and our friendly staff are always on hand to help with any questions or concerns. With affordable admission prices and plenty of parking, Kot Bury Ferm is the perfect destination for a day out with the family. Don't wait – plan your visit to Kot Bury Ferm today and experience the ultimate in amusement park fun!

The Ranch
At Kot Bury Ferm, we don't just offer thrilling rides and attractions - we're also home to a variety of adorable animals at our zoo! From fluffy bunnies and cute pigs to majestic horses and curious cows, our zoo is a favorite among visitors of all ages. You can get up close and personal with the animals and learn about their habitats, behavior, and diet. Our experienced staff are on hand to answer any questions you may have, and they're always happy to share fascinating facts and stories about our animal friends. If you're looking for a unique animal experience, why not try our goat milking or egg collecting activities? You'll get to interact with our friendly goats and chickens, and you'll even get to take home the fresh eggs you collect! Or, if you prefer a more hands-off approach, you can simply stroll through the zoo and admire the animals from a distance. One of our most popular exhibits is our baby animal nursery, where you can see some of the cutest and most adorable newborns up close. From tiny piglets to fluffy chicks, there's nothing quite as heartwarming as watching these little ones play and explore in their new surroundings. So come on down to Kot Bury Ferm and experience the joy of our zoo for yourself. You're sure to leave with a smile on your face and a newfound appreciation for the beauty and wonder of nature.
